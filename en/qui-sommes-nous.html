About My Learning.

What is the objective of the platform?

The objective of My Learning is to ensure complementarity in the course of primary students in high schools. This platform provides complementary courses.

Is My Learning free?

Indeed, My Learning is a completely free platform accessible to all students from a computer.

Can we download the courses?

This platform allows you to download the courses and all this for free, this download then allows you to access them without internet connection.

Who is the founder of the project?

This platform is developed and is managed by two general students (isn 2020 project).


My Learning is an internet platform aimed at helping students in need of additional resources, bringing together the different subjects of the core curriculum and specialty options.
